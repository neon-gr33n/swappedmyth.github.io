Swapped Myth (stylized as SWAPPED MYTH) is an Underswap take that aims to focus on expanding the world of Undertale/Deltarune,
with ideas both new and old, to enrich its world.

It is our interpretation/view of the premise of UNDERSWAP an alternate universe of Undertale in which character roles, personalities, or even locationsre swapped, in addition, we are adding original content, introducing new characters, while balancing the tone and creating our own unique identity

A DEMO is currently in development and will feature the first area, the Lucent Caverns!
